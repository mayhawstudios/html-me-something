My Trip to China 2016

Introduction

In college, I didn't have any options to study abroad. One of my majors didn't have any places to travel, and the other one did, but I didn't have much money at the time and when an opportunity did come up, I was already in my last semester before graduating. When I finally found a job and started my career, one of my sisters was planning on getting married while the other one got a chance to go abroad to China. I thought that my chance to do something exciting and new had passed me by, but then I was told I could pay to be a chapperone and I lept at the chance! After several months of payment, passport and visa applications, I finally got to go someplace amazing!
